Chuck's Favorite Movies

Halloween Edition

An American Werewolf In London

Probably the best film werewolf transformation ever. Griffin Dunne's supporting role is also a highlight.

Shaun of the Dead

Hilarious film with a great soundtrack and brilliant editing. Many great one-liners, "You've got red on you."

The Exorcist

Amazing acting performances from all especially the young Linda Blair. Also amazing and horrifying special effects far in advance of CGI technology.

The Shining

Not one of Stephen King's favored adaptations of his work but Kubrick's version of the story is a classic. Jack Nicholson's axe weilding scene in the bathroom is legendary.

The Thing

Amazing and gruesome effects. I love Kurt Russell in this very suspenseful and tense film.
